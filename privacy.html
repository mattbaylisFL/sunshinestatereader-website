Privacy Policy

Sunshine State Reader

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Sunshine State Reader does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information from its users.

All reading progress, settings, and achievements are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer.

External Links

The app contains links to Amazon and WorldCat (library search). These links open in your device's default web browser. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. All external links require a parent/guardian verification step before opening.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed to be used by children under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else.
